About

The development of music recommendation systems using ontologies and web semantics has gained significant attention in recent years. By analyzing user preferences, behavior, and past listening habits, these systems suggest songs, albums, or artists to users that align with their individual preferences. In this project, we have designed a music recommendation system that recommends popular songs based on a chosen genre or artist using their popularity score. Our system is able to identify patterns and provide personalized recommendations that enhance the listening experience for music lovers.
